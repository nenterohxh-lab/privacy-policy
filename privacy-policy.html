Privacy Policy — ScanGo X

Last updated: 2025

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Camera Access

ScanGo X uses the device camera exclusively to scan QR codes and barcodes. The camera feed is processed in real time and is never stored, transmitted, or used for any other purpose.

1.2. User-Generated Content

All QR and barcode generation is processed locally on your device. No data is collected or transmitted.

1.3. Quick Actions

Optional actions such as adding contacts, opening links, or saving information are performed only at your request and do not involve data collection.

1.4. Advertising (AdMob)

ScanGo X uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google may collect the Advertising ID (AD_ID), device information, and interaction data with ads. Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

1.5. In-App Purchases

Premium purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. The developer does not receive personal information such as names, emails, or payment details.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used only to enable app features, display ads, process purchases, and perform user-requested actions.

3. Data Storage and Security

No personal data is stored on external servers. All sensitive operations occur locally on the device.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google Play Services

5. Permissions Used

  • Camera
  • Photos/Media
  • Internet
  • AD_ID
  • Notifications

6. Children’s Privacy

ScanGo X is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
