Privacy Policy — ScanGo X Last updated: 2025 This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application ScanGo X, developed by nentero, handles user information. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. 1. Information We Collect 1.1. Camera Access ScanGo X uses the device camera exclusively to scan QR codes and barcodes. The camera feed is processed in real time and is never stored, transmitted, or used for any other purpose. No images or video frames are saved or uploaded. 1.2. User-Generated Content When you generate QR codes or barcodes, all data is processed locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit this information. 1.3. Quick Actions ScanGo X includes optional quick actions such as adding contacts, opening links, sending messages, or saving information locally. These actions are performed only at your request and do not involve data collection by the developer. 1.4. Advertising (AdMob) ScanGo X uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google may collect the Advertising ID (AD_ID), device information, and interaction data with ads. Google processes this data according to its own Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy The developer does not receive personal data from AdMob. 1.5. In-App Purchases ScanGo X offers a one-time purchase to unlock premium features. Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing, which may collect information necessary to complete the transaction. The developer does not receive personal information such as names, emails, or payment details. 2. How We Use Information Information is used solely to enable scanning and generation features, display ads, process premium purchases, and execute user-requested actions. We do not sell, share, or transfer personal data to third parties. 3. Data Storage and Security ScanGo X does not store personal data on external servers. All sensitive operations occur locally on the device. Reasonable measures are applied to protect data processed within the app. 4. Third-Party Services ScanGo X may use the following services: - Google AdMob (advertising) - Google Play Billing (in-app purchases) - Google Play Services (core functionality) Each service has its own privacy policy and data practices. 5. Permissions Used ScanGo X may request the following permissions: - Camera: to scan QR codes and barcodes - Photos/Media: to import images for scanning - Internet: to load ads and online features - AD_ID: for advertising purposes - Notifications: for optional alerts Permissions are used only for their intended purpose. 6. Children’s Privacy ScanGo X is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. 7.
